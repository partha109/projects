About Us

The Alberta Tourism Agency is the official government organization responsible for promoting tourism in the beautiful Canadian province of Alberta. Established to showcase the diverse and captivating landscapes, attractions, and experiences that Alberta has to offer, the agency plays a crucial role in driving the province's tourism industry.

Alberta is renowned for its majestic Rocky Mountains, including famous destinations such as Banff and Jasper National Parks. Travel Alberta works tirelessly to ensure that visitors from around the world get to witness the inspiring beauty of these mountain landscapes. The agency is also committed to showcasing Alberta's unique cultural heritage. Visitors can explore the vibrant city life in places like Calgary and Edmonton, where they can enjoy a rich blend of arts, culture, and culinary delights.

One of the agency's core strengths is its promotion of outdoor activities. Alberta is a playground for adventurers, offering activities such as hiking, skiing, kayaking, and camping. Travel Alberta provides extensive resources, guides, and recommendations to help tourists plan their outdoor escapades. Moreover, the agency emphasizes sustainability and responsible tourism. Alberta's pristine natural environments are treasures worth protecting, and Travel Alberta strives to ensure that tourism leaves a minimal ecological footprint. By promoting eco-friendly practices and encouraging travelers to respect the environment, Travel Alberta plays a significant role in preserving the province's stunning landscapes. The Alberta Tourism Agency is your trusted guide to a remarkable journey through this captivating province.
